Overview

Purpose

Do you love animation, creativity, and adorable animals? So does Happy Spark, and the 400 others that are subscribed to her! However, despite the years she’s had on YouTube and the following she has, we are yet to get much background. Who are these colorful characters she’s created? Well, wonder no longer! I, a two year computer programmer studying html, will be working side by side with Happy Spark, to give you the most accurate, and detailed profiles she has and more, all on one, adorable, easy access website! Our hope is the give viewers the context they’ve been looking for, to make all her animations easier to understand and much more meaningful. The untold stories will finally be told!

Audience

The main audiance of this site would obviosly be viewers of her channel which can range from any age, though youtube is officially for 12+. "Family-friendly" has always been my sisters main goal, and her style has always been colorful and cute. Therefore, I expect this to be the exact themes our aubdience will be looking for in the site. This encourages bright pastel, like colors, and cute fonts. The webite should be simple, and not overly flashy, as that is what best matches the art style HappySpark uses in her animations. The website will also be aiming to please, mostly, the curious viewers, who are looking for a little more from her content. If they are visiting this site, they are likely looking for context and background info about the characters they often see, their history, and how they are connected.

Typography

Heading Font: Hi Melody

Handwritten Fonts are what I will be using 100%, as it matches the theme of her animations, also being hand-drawn. I chose this one specificlly, as it uses bold and rounded edges, which matches the art style she uses. She almost never uses angled corners, and uses bold outlines.

Paragraph Font: Gaegu

I chose this one for mostly the same reason as the headlines. It matches her art style, but is also a little less flashy, which I feel is an important quality for paragraphs to have. They should feel natural, unlike headlines, which need to stand out. The writing of this font also feels a little sloppier than the headlines, which I think creates a more handmade feel Usually when someone writes notes, They'll be a lot more careful and neat with they titles, but sloppier with the actually writing becasue there is a lot more to put down.
