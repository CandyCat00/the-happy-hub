Origins

Mantis is an alien from an unknown planet. No one knows where she came from, she simply just fell from the sky one day. Ginji was the first to find her, as she almost crashed right on top of him. He took her in, at first with only the intention of getting rid of her as soon as possible, but has gradually become quite fond of her as the days go by. She has a child like intellect, and is extremely curious about everything she finds. She makes everyone smile with her excitement to learn and experiment. Especially when it comes to dancing.
