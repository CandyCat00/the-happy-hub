Yasashi

Basic Info

Species: Corgi
Coat: Blue
Eye Color: Yellow
Weight: 25lb

Background

Cheerful and upbeat, Yasashi’s first few years of her childhood was spent at “Puppy Pawz Orphange” after getting separated from her parents. Her blue fur made it hard for her to fit in, making her very self conscious about her look. However, when she is acquainted with others dear to her heart, she’s known to put their well being above her own. Her smile is quite contagious.
